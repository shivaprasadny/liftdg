# LiftDG privacy policy — project draft

This draft requires legal and store-policy review before publication.

LiftDG does not require an account and has no LiftDG-operated backend. Workout, exercise, plan, settings, and progress data are stored locally in the app's device storage. LiftDG does not sell workout data or upload it to a LiftDG server.

Users control JSON backups and CSV files they export. Once a file is shared, the chosen operating-system share destination may store or process it under that destination's policies. Device or operating-system backups may include LiftDG data depending on device settings.

Biometric authentication is performed by the device operating system. LiftDG stores only app-lock configuration and does not receive biometric templates. Removing the app or losing a device without an exported/device backup may permanently remove local data.
## Profile and body measurements

Names, dates of birth, height, body weight, and body measurements are stored locally in LiftDG's on-device SQLite database. LiftDG does not upload this information and does not use it for medical diagnosis or health recommendations. No account is required. Exported JSON or CSV files are controlled by the user, are readable files, and may expose this information to anyone who can access the selected share destination or file. The existing device-authentication app lock protects these screens while the app is locked; it is not a second encryption system.
