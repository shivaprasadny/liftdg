# Play Store listing draft

- App name: LiftDG
- Short description: Offline strength and cardio tracking. Build plans and track every rep.

LiftDG tracks strength workouts, cardio, reusable plans, advanced sets, history, personal records, and progress entirely on device. Log sets, repetitions and weight; perform supersets, drop sets and circuits; and create JSON backups or CSV reports you control. No account is required.

Privacy summary: LiftDG has no backend and does not upload workout data. Android biometric permission is used only when app lock is enabled.

Screenshot plan: Home; Exercise Library; Plan; Active Workout; Advanced Group; History; Progress; Cardio; Settings/Backup. Capture real phone screenshots after final visual QA.
